Our Story

It all started with… a roommate application. Da’Quan was just having another normal day and Savannah was just trying to sign her very first apartment lease. But God had other plans. Savannah was in a rush to sign her new lease because all the good apartments were getting taken. Desperate not to lose out on another apartment, she decided to meet up with her roommate at her current location. However, her roommate mentioned she had just finished working out with a friend.

That friend happened to be one of Da’Quan’s best friends and current roommate. Fate brought the two together through a series of coincidental events. All it took for him was one gaze to know he had to figure out who this never-before-seen woman was. With little hesitation, he asked for Savannah’s Instagram, and the story began.

What started as a single DM turned into hours of conversation, a first date filled with laughter, deep talks, and Da’Quan sweetly calling her “beloved.” Savannah, naturally shy, was stunned by how easy it felt—like she’d known him forever. From there, things escalated quickly in the best way. Love letters and random flower deliveries became a regular thing. And yes, Da’Quan somehow found a dress that Savannah had only casually mentioned—without any description—and surprised her with it. He even rewrote her résumé to help her land a new job, and woke up at 3 a.m. to make sure her car didn’t get towed.

Meanwhile, Savannah showed her love in her own thoughtful ways—making homemade lunches for him to take to work, preparing a full recovery program to help him rehab his ongoing knee injuries, and even FaceTiming him to make sure he did his workouts (sometimes joining in herself). Her kindness, warmth, and authenticity spoke straight to Da’Quan’s heart. He knew she was The One when multiple people randomly asked why he looked so happy lately. The answer was evident to him. That was all he needed to know.

Eventually, the moment came. Not the moment (yet), but a pretty important one. At a cozy little restaurant—with cheesecake, candles, and “Will you be my girlfriend?” written out in chocolate syrup on a dessert plate (Savannah having made it clear, a few times, that time was ticking)—Da’Quan made it official. Roses included. That night marked the start of something even bigger than either of them imagined: a love story full of laughter, long talks that never feel long enough, a strong bond, and most importantly, a relationship built on faith and Jesus. And now, they can’t wait to continue their never-ending chapter together.
